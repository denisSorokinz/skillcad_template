About Us

SKILLCAD is dedicated to user-guided auto routing and productivity enhancement tools for custom IC layout design, providing correct and optimized by construction layout solutions to help design teams achieve tape out deadlines while improving silicon yields.


SKILLCAD Inc. is a member of Cadence Design System Connections Program for third party developers. Presently, SKILLCAD tools run as an advanced add-on module in the Virtuoso platform of Cadence Design System, supporting IC6, IC12, IC18 and IC5
