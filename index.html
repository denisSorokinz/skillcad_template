About Us
SKILLCAD is dedicated to user-guided auto routing and productivity enhancement tools for custom IC layout design, providing correct and optimized by construction layout solutions to help customers achieve shorter time-to-market and higher silicon yield. SKILLCAD Inc. is a member of Cadence Design System Connections Program for third party developers. Presently, SKILLCAD tools run as an advanced add-on module in the Virtuoso platform of Cadence Design System, supporting IC6, IC12,IC18 and IC5
